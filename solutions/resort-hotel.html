The solution

One tram. One driver.
A guest experience they remember.

FlexTram is a high-capacity guest shuttle designed for resort and hotel properties. Where you once needed three vans and three drivers, a single FlexTram handles up to 27 guests per trip — with one driver — on a continuous, predictable loop.

Guests always know where to board and when. No calling the front desk, no waiting for a van. The tram runs a fixed route between pools, restaurants, parking, and amenities — turning transportation into part of the resort experience.

FlexTram is ADA accessible as standard, stores compactly when seasonal demand drops, and costs a fraction of what a multi-van fleet runs. Operators consistently report 50-60% reductions in shuttle labor costs.

Common questions

Can FlexTram replace our hotel shuttle van fleet?

Yes. One FlexTram carries up to 27 guests — more than double a standard shuttle van. Most resort properties replace 3-4 vans with 1-2 FlexTrams while cutting driver labor by 50% or more.

Is FlexTram better than golf carts for resort guest transport?

Golf carts carry 4-6 guests and aren't ADA accessible. FlexTram carries up to 27 guests with full ADA compliance, runs a predictable loop, and requires one driver instead of a fleet of cart operators.

Can FlexTram run a continuous loop around our resort property?

Yes — FlexTram is designed for fixed-route continuous loops. With 8-10 hours of range per charge, it runs all day between pools, restaurants, parking, and amenities on a schedule guests can count on.

Is FlexTram ADA accessible for resort guests with mobility needs?

ADA accessibility is standard on every FlexTram — not an add-on. Accessible boarding, appropriate aisle widths, and additional configurations are available on request.

Can we scale our FlexTram fleet for peak season?

Absolutely. FlexTram stores compactly and requires no dedicated infrastructure. Add trams for high season, remove them when occupancy drops — no long-term fleet commitment required.
