The problem

What the parking lot walk
is costing your guest experience

The parking lot hike
Guests with kids, strollers, and elderly family members walk 10-15 minutes from remote lots. They arrive tired and frustrated before the fun begins.
Golf cart chaos
Dozens of 6-seat golf carts weaving through parking lots create safety hazards and visual clutter — not the branded experience guests expect.
High driver headcount
A 20-cart fleet needs 20 drivers. That's a massive labor cost for a parking lot operation — and most carts run half-empty.
Accessibility gaps
Families with wheelchair users or guests with mobility challenges are underserved by golf carts. The parking lot becomes a barrier to your park.

The solution

A branded tram guests remember.
From parking to front gate.

FlexTram is a high-capacity theme park tram that transforms the parking lot shuttle from a pain point into part of the guest experience. One branded tram carries up to 27 guests — families, strollers, wheelchairs — from remote lots to the front gate with one driver.

Where a 20-cart fleet needs 20 drivers, five FlexTrams handle the same guest volume with five drivers. The parking lot is cleaner, safer, and more organized. Guests ride a predictable loop instead of hoping a cart spots them in the lot.

FlexTram is ADA accessible as standard, compact, and can be branded with your park's identity. It scales for peak season and stores compactly in the off-season.

Common questions

Can FlexTram replace golf carts in theme park parking lots?

Yes. One FlexTram replaces 4-5 golf carts, carrying up to 27 guests on a branded, predictable loop from parking to front gate. Most parks reduce cart fleets by 70% or more.

Can FlexTram be branded with our park's identity?

Absolutely. FlexTram supports custom branding, wraps, and theming. Many parks turn the tram into a photo opportunity and part of the guest experience.

Is FlexTram ADA accessible for theme park guests?

ADA accessibility is standard. Accessible boarding accommodates wheelchairs, strollers, and guests with mobility challenges — no separate ADA vehicle needed.

Can FlexTram scale for peak season at a theme park?

Yes. Add trams for summer peak, holiday events, and special occasions. Store them compactly during the off-season. No year-round fleet commitment.

How does FlexTram improve the guest experience vs. walking from parking?

Guests arrive at the front gate fresh and excited instead of tired and frustrated. A branded, predictable tram loop turns what was a pain point into the first positive touchpoint of their visit.
