The problem

What scattered golf carts
are really costing your operation

Tournament day gridlock
144 golfers arrive in a 90-minute window. Golf carts carry 4 at a time. Getting everyone from parking to the first tee is a logistical nightmare.
Clubhouse access for non-golfers
Event guests, banquet attendees, and dining patrons aren't comfortable driving golf carts — but walking from remote parking isn't acceptable either.
Staff scattered across the property
Maintenance crews, food service, and event staff need reliable transport across the property. Dedicating golf carts to staff means fewer for members.
Accessibility gaps
Members and guests with mobility challenges can't use standard golf carts. Your property isn't fully accessible — and that limits your audience.

The solution

One tram for transport.
Golf carts for golf.

FlexTram separates your transportation needs from your golf fleet. A single compact tram runs a predictable loop between parking, clubhouse, pro shop, and course access points — carrying up to 27 passengers with one driver.

On tournament days, FlexTram handles the surge — moving 144 golfers from parking to the first tee in a fraction of the time it takes with golf carts. For banquets and events, non-golfer guests ride comfortably without needing to drive a cart.

FlexTram is ADA accessible as standard, quiet enough for the golf environment, and stores compactly in the off-season. Your golf carts stay on the course where they belong.

Common questions

Can FlexTram handle tournament day logistics at a golf course?

Yes. FlexTram carries up to 27 golfers per trip from parking to the first tee. For a 144-player shotgun start, a single FlexTram can clear the parking lot in a fraction of the time it takes with golf carts.

Is FlexTram quiet enough for a golf course environment?

Yes. FlexTram operates quietly — comparable to a golf cart — making it appropriate for the golf course environment without disturbing play.

Can non-golfers ride FlexTram to banquets and events?

Absolutely. FlexTram is designed for all guests — not just golfers. Banquet attendees, dining patrons, and event guests ride comfortably without needing to drive a golf cart. ADA accessibility is standard.

Can FlexTram navigate golf course terrain?

Yes. FlexTram handles cart paths, grass, gravel, and mixed surfaces. It navigates the same terrain as golf carts while carrying significantly more passengers.

Can we rent FlexTram for tournament weekends only?

Yes. FlexTram offers single-event rentals, seasonal leases, and long-term options. Use it for tournament weekends, member events, or daily operations — scale to your needs.
