Built to move.
Engineered for flexibility.

FlexTram outperforms sprinter vans and golf carts across every operation that moves people at scale. Fewer vehicles, fewer drivers, lower liability — and a transportation line your passengers can count on.

And with our Smart Stops, during slower times, FlexTrams don't run until a customer requests a ride. Push a button, operator receives a notification, tram is dispatched. Service on demand.

Why FlexTram

Lower labor costs
One driver per tram replaces multiple drivers across a van or cart fleet.
Fewer vehicles, less liability
Consolidate your fleet and reduce insurance exposure across your operation.
Pop-up ready
Deploy for one-day events, seasonal surges, or temporary operations with no permanent infrastructure.
Predictable transport loop
Passengers always know where to board and when — no hailing, no waiting on a random van.
Easy to store
Compact footprint means FlexTram parks and stores without dedicated infrastructure.
Scale up or down
