FlexTram Atlas

Practitioner references.

An atlas is not a blog post and not a solution page. It's a catalog — structured, sourced, and maintained — of the named entities that matter most in a vertical. Acreage, capacity, ownership, current operational state, and the kind of practitioner commentary that doesn't appear in vendor brochures.

Two atlases published so far. More are planned as each format proves out under usage.

Destination resorts · Updated July 20, 2026
Large-footprint destination resorts
Eighteen large-footprint U.S. destination resorts grouped by ownership platform — Anschutz (Sea Island, Broadmoor), South Street (Kiawah, Palmetto Bluff), Omni, KemperSports, Salamander — plus the single-property giants (Greenbrier, Pinehurst, Big Cedar, Sandestin, French Lick, Sea Pines, Nemacolin). Acreage, lodging scale, amenity dispersion, and current onsite transit per entry.
